What is your approach to web design?

We have extensive experience working with world-renowned brands following a super creative web design process for 12 years. We have a few hottest website design tips that make your goals achievable. Our approach to web design is divided into seven different stages: 

Identification of Purpose & Goals: 

We closely interact with the client to know what their website wants them to fulfill. 

Coverage Definition:

Here we'll know:

  • What kind of website does the client want: Template-Based or Customized?
  • How many web pages are there? 
  • What kind of Content & Visual Representation is the client demanding? 
  • Within how much time the client wants its website to be ready? 

Web Design:

Now it's time to turn ideas into reality. A well-planned website structure and visual representation are delivered at this stage. Here are the inclusions of the designing stage: 

  • Wireframe And Elements Designing
  • Creation of Graphics
  • Mock-Ups As Required
  • Reviews And Approvals
  • Code Valid XHTML/CSS

Web Development:

Now the design stage will end, and we will start with complex website development codes to create a functional and user-friendly website. We will develop your Website after considering certain essential factors:

  • Choice of Platform: WordPress, Magento, Shopify, Woo Commerce, Laravel etc.
  • Database: The development of a logical & robust database for the smooth performance of the Website will be our primary focus.
  • Features: We will ensure your Website accomplishes all the latest parts and functionalities.
  • Content:  Our professional content writers make sure you have suitable material for your Website 

Creation of Sitemaps:

Now that we have a clear picture of the project's coverage, we can start thinking about the sitemap to determine how the features and content we defined in the scope will interact.

Testing:

By now, we will get everything ready. So at this stage, we do testing to ensure everything is working well. 

Launch: 

Once the testing has reported your site to be flawless, we plan to launch your site.
